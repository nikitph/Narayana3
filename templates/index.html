Hi you. Hello from Naryna!
Naryna is a CBT tool to capture your cognitive distortions. We aim to utilize the latest advancements in Data science and machine learning along with a little bit of crowdsourcing magic to automatically refute your negative thoughts and cognitive distortions. We also provide a depression tracking tool to keep a track of various aspects of depression over time.

Important Update

Nov 2015 : Naryna will soon be acqu-hire-merging. Beginning Jan 2016, Naryna as an entity will shut down. New signups will be closed by the end of next month. The service will however remain operational through 2017 for existing users. Bug fixes will be suspended from Jan 2016. We remain committed to releasing our iOS app but it will now probably see the light of the day through one of our individual contributors and with only offline functionality. It will still hold the moniker Naryna. We *may* open source a limited part of our codebase.

But Why? : We started Naryna to help people with depression. We were just not able to expand enough to help people at a scale we wanted to. But suffice it to say that Naryna will live on as a part of a much grander and magnificent company which prefers to remain in stealth at the moment. Thank you for your love and support. -- Naryna Team.

Update, Oct 2016 : As promised, one of our Founder & Individual contributors (Nikit Phadke) has released the iphone app. It's very basic, doesn't have any ML jazz and mostly offline. Hope it helps some of you.
